INTERIOR STYLING TIPS

Decorating with Cushions

Scatter cushions, an affordable way to refresh a room, add color and depth. At Henry and Oliver Co, we advise selecting a color theme that aligns with your existing décor and building around it with mixed patterns, sizes, and textures. Skillful arrangement on your bed or sofa brings balance, not necessarily symmetry. With our wide variety of cushions, our team is ready to help you create an enticing and cohesive look for your space.

Stain on Your Hampton’s Lounge? Here’s How to Remove it.

Accidents happen, even to your sumptuous cream Hampton-style lounge. Whether it's a cat mishap, a child's chocolate-covered hand, or a guest's spilt wine, stains are inevitable. When they occur, it's crucial to act swiftly for the best chance at full removal. Consider investing in lounge covers or fabric protection as a preventive measure to keep your furniture looking its best.

Latest colour trend in Hamptons Styling...

Embrace the refreshing Hamptons style, traditionally known for its crisp whites, neutral greys, and coastal blues, now evolving to include richer, warmer tones. Our current trend incorporates chocolate brown furniture, creamy beiges, dusty pinks, tropical greens, and cool coastal blues with bursts of gold for a complete, playful look. It's a fusion of a relaxed coastal vibe and warm tropical islands, ideal for the Australian summer. Henry and Oliver's new collection offers cushions featuring tropical prints and coastal homewares, creating a unique blend of forest and sea. For a stylish, contemporary home makeover, explore our new product range.

Create that Hamptons Look for Smaller Spaces

Achieving the spacious, airy Hamptons style in smaller spaces is entirely possible with some clever tricks. To create an illusion of a bigger room, opt for soft, light-coloured walls and cabinetry, which reflect light and brighten up the space. Enhance this effect with high-quality features like marble benchtops and striking pendant lights, and use wall mirrors to further amplify light. Choose bright, neutral-toned bulbs for artificial lighting, but if you have good natural light, consider warmer bulbs. Embrace the coastal-meets-luxe Hamptons style by using natural materials like rattan, wood, and wicker, alongside white or white-washed surfaces, and add pops of color with decor and homewares for a bolder look. For personalized advice on achieving this style, contact us for a consultation.
